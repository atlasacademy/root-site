With the recent LostBelt 6.3 leak, some has criticized our actions as hypocritical with all the leaks we have been supplying. This post is to clarify our goal and where we see our intent.

Our overall goal is to provide data to people who want to create content for FGO. Our mined data powers websites, bots, translators, theory crafters, artists, and just the curious. Leaking upcoming changes, and gaining access to parts of the game early is a by-product of us providing more comprehensive services for our users and is not the original intent.

If a leak were to impact our overall mission, then we will decide to not do so. Releasing hints about upcoming features is one thing, but providing the general public will the full chapter is another. Some might argue it happened anyways with other miners releasing the content. However we had much more. We had everything to the point we could have made a start to finish video of all of LostBelt 6.3 before it was even released. Deciding a line in what assets are appropriate to leak or not is not something we wanted to figure out and decided to not release any of it.

Hope that clears things up.
