New and veteran players alike may wonder how to clear these quests. The least expensive setups are solos, and present a clear baseline for all other clears. Conversely, they offer somewhat painless ways to approach these quests for players with undeveloped rosters. With that in mind, and with the help of friend supports and some of the showcased videos, I'm sure any player could tackle these challenges face-on.

For an in-depth explanation of the various gimmicks employed in these CQs, please refer to  
<strong>[Battle in New York 2020's Fandom Wikia page](https://fategrandorder.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_in_New_York_2020_(US))</strong>

Please note that this sheet doesn't consist of farming comps, we recommend checking <strong>[SaberOfAvalon & xzero's sheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1wINTpaUufAXwDci9NXTxkWhdIBmjAa6p5qkSYYbmgfU/htmlview)</strong> for farming comps instead. You can also check #resources on Atlas Academy's Discord server for more links.
