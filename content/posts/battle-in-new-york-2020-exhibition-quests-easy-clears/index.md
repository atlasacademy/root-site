New and veteran players alike may wonder how to clear the numerous Challenge Quests that arrive with the titular event. The least expensive setups are solos, and present a clear baseline for all other clears.  
Conversely, they offer a somewhat painless way to approach these quests for players with undeveloped rosters. With that in mind, and with the help of friend supports and some of the showcased videos, I'm sure any player could tackle these challenges face-on.<br>  
    For an in-depth explanation of the various gimmicks employed in these CQs, please refer to [Battle in New York 2020's Fandom Wikia page](https://fategrandorder.fandom.com/wiki/Battle_in_New_York_2020_(US)).  
    Please note that this sheet doesn't offer farming comps. For those, we recommend checking [SaberOfAvalon & xzero's sheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1wINTpaUufAXwDci9NXTxkWhdIBmjAa6p5qkSYYbmgfU/htmlview) for farming comps instead.  
    You can also check `#resources` on the [Atlas Academy's Discord server](/discord/) for more links.

Flame Gate is somewhat challenging to solo due to Saber Shiki and Nero being the only two servants capable of doing it. However, it remains relatively easy to do with any AoE Saber and appropriate supports.
