Atlas Academy was initially a group for maintain drop sheets and other crowd-sourced data for the FGO-NA community. Over time, the team grew and took on more projects. Our goal is to gather and serve information for everyone who enjoys FGO as much as we do.

Please feel free to reach out to us on [Discord](https://discord.gg/7jBA5FZdEF) to contact us.
